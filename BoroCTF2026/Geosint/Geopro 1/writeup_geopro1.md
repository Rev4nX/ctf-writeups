## Description

> Find the city this image was taken in.
> Flag format: boroCTF{city_name} (Replace any space with an underscore)

## Files

- `geosint.png` — aerial/ground photo of ornate Indo-Saracenic architecture

---

## Solution

Upload `geosint.png` to **Google** using reverse image search.

Result: **Lakshmi Vilas Palace**, Vadodara, India — one of the largest private palaces in the world, built in Indo-Saracenic style in 1890.

boroCTF{Vadodara}

## Key takeaways

- Reverse image search (Google) is the first and often only step needed for image-based GEOSINT
- For architecture, Google Lens identifies the specific building and its location almost instantly
